October Technological University (OTU)

October Technological University (OTU) is part of Egypt's national initiative to expand technological education and align academic outcomes with labor market needs. This university, located in the 6th of October City, offers a practical, hands-on approach to higher education, focusing on applied sciences and technology.

Facilities and Industry Collaboration

OTU collaborates with local industries and international organizations to ensure students gain relevant experience through internships, industry projects, and certifications like Cisco Networking. It provides state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced laboratories, workshops, and smart classrooms, to prepare students for emerging challenges in technology-driven fields.

Vision and Impact

The university supports Egypt's vision for digital transformation and sustainable development by focusing on innovative fields such as renewable energy, cybersecurity, and industrial automation. OTU's balanced education system ensures that graduates are equipped with practical skills and theoretical knowledge, making them competitive in both local and global job markets.
